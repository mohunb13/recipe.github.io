Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • Self-rising flour
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Melted butter

METHOD

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Also add vanilla and melted butter
  5. Add milk and make it a batter consistency
  6. Pour the mixture into the greased pan
  7. Bake for 20 minutes
  8. Cool over a wire rack
